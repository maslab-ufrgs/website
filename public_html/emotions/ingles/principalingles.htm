Personalities and Emotions in Agents
The simulation is a powerful tool to help the decision process, where, through an artificial reproduction, called model, the problem under consideration studied and the behavior is observed. Using the computer for simulation is extremely important, because the process becomes faster, more accurated and cheaper. For the great power of evaluation, flexibility and versatility, the computational simulation finds application in practically all the areas of the activity human being. 
Multi-agents Systems have very important characteristics, as autonomy, flexibility, collaboration and adaptation, which are useful for a lot of applications. Due to these characteristics, an area that can be benefited with the help of this technology is the simulation. The use of this technology can bring excellent results to this area because, for the modeling, each agent represents an element of the problem. 

We believe that, the inclusion of emotions in the participant agents of the simulation environment can bring improvement to its functionalities. To simulate emotions as anger, love, joy, etc. can demonstrate relative behaviors to these, as hostility to the cooperation with other agents. To use emotions in the agents involves a form of less trivial reasoning, becoming the agents more complex.
