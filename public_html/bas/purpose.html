Purpose


Agents and multiagent systems (AAMAS) is one of the most attractive research areas in Artificial Intelligence at the moment. The interest is growing and MAS-related meetings nowadays attract researchers and students from all over the world.

In order to disseminate the knowledge about AAMAS, the IFMAS, AA, and ATAL sponsor a series of schools. Recent editions were in China and India.

The purpose of the school in Porto Alegre is to bring together graduate students and also professionals interested in agents technologies and research on multiagent systems living in South America. However, everyone is welcome.

Depending on the number of potential participants, there will be a selection process based on the Vitae and how the school can benefit the participant. Please go to the registration page for further instructions.
