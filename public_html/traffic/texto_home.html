Problem:
  • increasing demand for mobility in modern societies.... jams!
Possible solutions:
  • better utilization of the existing infrastructure
  • intelligent transportation systems (ITS)
  • information guidance, advanced travellers information system (ATIS)
Our goals:
  • a multidisciplinary approach to ITS
  • use of artificial intelligence, multiagent systems for traffic management, control and simulation
  • development of an open-source microscopic traffic simulator (basic infrastructure)
  • use of the simulator to test approaches to:
    • descentralized control of traffic signals
    • route choice with richer models of drivers (mental states, preferences, etc.)
